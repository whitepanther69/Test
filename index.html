Avitel Mission

At Avitel, we specialize in design, engineering, programming, installing, and maintaining smart home technology and commercial automation systems. Our expertise lies in providing seamless control of integrated technology and automation systems, encompassing audio/video, lighting, motorized shades, climate control, energy and power management, networking, and surveillance/security systems. Since our inception in 1998, our mission has remained steadfast: to provide individuals and businesses with innovative technology solutions that simplify and enrich their lives. We have served the greater Phoenix area and numerous out-of-state projects. Our greatest source of pride lies in the strong partnerships we have made with our trade partners and loyal customers, many of whom have been with us since the beginning. We extend a warm invitation to you to experience the Avitel difference and become part of our family. Welcome to Avitel, where innovation meets unparalleled service.
